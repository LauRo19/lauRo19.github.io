WHEN CAN ANIMATIONS HURT USER EXPERIENCE?

Normally animations are charming and very engaging, but sometimes, when they are bad designed and don't have any purpose they become into something annoying and useless.

But what happens when, instead of having one animation, whether is well designed or not, there is many? What would you do if everything is moving at the same time on the same screen? Would you know what you have to click-on first? This situation is actually making your experience harder than it should. So if you are planning on creating any type of website or phone application, is imperative for you to balance the kind and quantity of animations you want to add to your design with the user experience you want to offer.

Here are some disadvantages that can hurt the user experience and that your should consider while creating your site.

Having a lot of animations in just one site usually takes longer to load, making the process frustating. Animations that are merely created for decoration should have a fuction, but if they don't have any is better to get rid off them than risking the design's functionality. If the user has an outdated browser and you website contains a lot or heavy animations most probably the user wont be able to enter or use your site. Sometimes, animations can distract from what the focal point, but if your animations is necessary for your site and enhances a positive experience, is recomended to make it subtle and simple.
