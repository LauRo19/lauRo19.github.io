My name is Laura Mendez, I am a 26 years old designer, originally from Colombia, who recently graduated from MassArt and loves creating new stuff. I'm very interested in everything that is related to graphic design, both in digital and printed media.

I truly believe that in order to create great things, one's creative process needs to go through a series of stages; from researching, sketching, gathering feedback, refining, curating and finally presenting. All with the same level of relevance and value.

Designing doesn't necessarily defines who you are, but it certainly shows the creative part of you.
